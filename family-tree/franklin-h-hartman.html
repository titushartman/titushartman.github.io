Franklin H. HARTMAN was a grand-uncle of Titus HARTMAN. Franklin H. and Amanda K. HARTMAN are buried together with their children Carrie L. and Russell L. at Fairview Cemetery in Boyertown, PA.

Along with her cousins Verna HARTMAN (daughter of William Hafer HARTMAN) and Charles G. HARTMAN (son of Daniel H. HARTMAN), Carrie was a victim of the Rhoads Opera House fire in Boyertown on January 13, 1908.
